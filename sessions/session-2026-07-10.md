## Key finding (why this was small)
The brain already ran `_hb_pbuffs`/`_hb_mana_feed` for EVERY profile — they're in the
`_run_heartbeats` tuple and gated purely by config (`pbuff_N_interval_s` + a mapped key),
NOT by profile kind. `/api/dirge/buffs`, `/api/dirge/buff/{n}/execute`, and the pbuff
tunables likewise read the active profile regardless of kind. The ONLY thing locking
healers out was the UI: the recast `<section>` carried `dirge-only` (hidden for healers)
and `pollRecast()` bailed on `profileKind !== "dirge"`. So this was mostly un-gating +
adding the pbuff roles to the healer profiles.
