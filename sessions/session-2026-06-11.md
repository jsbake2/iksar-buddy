## Input injection ‚Äî KEY FINDING (load-bearing)
- **QEMU-level input injection is unreliable on this setup.** `virsh send-key`
  worked at first (login form) then stopped; `input-send-event` (QMP mouse) is
  accepted by QEMU but never moves the pointer (QXL/SPICE quirk; not the SPICE
  client ‚Äî confirmed with no client + no vdagent). Do NOT rely on it.
- **In-guest AutoHotkey is THE injection method** ‚Äî native Windows SendInput,
  100% reliable, and it's exactly what the bot agent will use at runtime.
  AHK v2 at `C:\ib\ahk\AutoHotkey64.exe`.
- **To launch a GUI/input process in the interactive session (1) from the agent
  (session 0):** `Register-ScheduledTask` with a `New-ScheduledTaskPrincipal
  -LogonType Interactive` principal for `iksar`, then `Start-ScheduledTask`.
  PsExec -i 1 gave "Access denied"; schtasks /it `/run` gave "Element not found".
  The Register-ScheduledTask Interactive route works ‚Äî used it to click "I ACCEPT".
